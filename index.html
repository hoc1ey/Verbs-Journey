These are the cards that will be in the game, here you can familiarize with them. Each one of them has a score (by default it's 15).

1. I didn't even bother ____ (ask).

Respuesta:

I didn't even bother TO ASK / ASKING.

Value: 15

2. I hate ____ (do) the dishes!

Respuesta:

I hate TO DO / DOING the dishes!

Value: 15

3. I prefer ____ (work) in teams.

Respuesta:

I prefer TO WORK / WORKING in teams.

Value: 15

4. We can't continue ____ (ignore) the Earth's issues.

Respuesta:

We can't continue TO IGNORE / IGNORING the Earth's issues.

Value: 15

5. Chris likes ____ (go out).

Respuesta:

Chris likes TO GO OUT / GOING OUT.

Value: 15

6. You need to start ____ (study) harder.

Respuesta:

You need to start TO STUDY / STUDYING harder.

Value: 15

7. I can't bear ____ (see) homeless people or animals.

Respuesta:

I can't bear TO SEE / SEEING homeless people or animals.

Value: 15

8. They decided ____ (postpone) the meeting.

Respuesta:

They decided TO POSTPONE the meeting.

Value: 15

9. She enjoys ____ (read) mystery novels.

Respuesta:

She enjoys READING mystery novels.

Value: 15

10. We hope ____ (visit) Paris next year.

Respuesta:

We hope TO VISIT Paris next year.

Value: 15
